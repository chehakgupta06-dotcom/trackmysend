First Steps in the App

Getting started with our app is easy and straightforward. Follow these steps to take full control of your finances:

1. Welcome Screen

Explore the key features of the app as we guide you through a quick overview of what it offers.

2. Create Your Account

Sign up with your email or log in securely to start managing your budget today.

3. Set Your Initial Budget

Input your budget amount and time period (e.g., monthly, weekly, yearly) to get started.

4. Add Your First Transaction

Track your expenses or income by adding your first transaction with a few simple clicks.

5. Explore the Dashboard

View your budget progress, track spending trends, and stay on top of your financial goals.

6. Personalize Your Experience

Change the theme, select your preferred language, or dive deeper into analytics to make the app truly yours.
