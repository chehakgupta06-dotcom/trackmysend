General Guides

Start by setting your budget on the Budget page. Enter your budget amount, select a period (monthly/weekly/yearly), and set start/end dates. Then go to Transactions to start logging your expenses.

Your data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. It never leaves your device. You can reset all data anytime from the Transactions page using the Reset button.

User Account and Data

Yes! All your financial data is stored only in your browser's local storage. We do not store any data on external servers.
Currently TrackMySpend stores data locally per device. Cross-device sync is on our roadmap for a future update.
Clearing browser data will erase all your saved budgets, transactions, and bills. We recommend exporting your data before clearing.

Transactions & Bills

Go to the Transactions page, fill in the type (expense/income), amount, category, and description, then click Add Transaction.
Go to Transactions
Click the red microphone button on the Transactions page and say something like: "add expense 500 for food". The app will automatically fill in the fields.
Scroll down on the Transactions page to find the Pending Bills section. Add a bill name, amount, and due date. You'll get alerts when bills are due soon.
